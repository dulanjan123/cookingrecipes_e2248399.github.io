SIMPLE DEEP FRY FISH RECIPE WITHOUT EGG, FLOUR, BREADING

Have you ever tried a simple Sri Lankan style deep fry fish recipe in your kitchen? If not, here is the right time to give it a try on this delicious fish dish. Today, I am going to show you an easy way to cook a deep fry fish recipe without eggs, flour, or breading.

A few months ago I have posted a whole fish fry recipe that went viral. So, this time I am going to show you another way to deep fry the fish.

In Sri Lanka, people prepare a variety of dishes using fish. This deep fry fish is one of the famous recipes among them.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fish – 6 pieces (500g)
  • Turmeric powder – ½ teaspoon
  • Chili powder – ½ teaspoon
  • Salt – As you need (KETO People use the Himalayan Pink Salt)
  • Cooking oil – As needed (KETO People use the Organic Coconut Oil)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Take the fish pieces and rinse them well.
  2. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and fry the fish pieces.
  4. Fry the fish pieces until they turn golden as shown in the picture below. If you wish, decorate them with onions, green chilies, and lime.
  5. This is how to make a simple deep fry fish recipe without egg or flour. Serve and enjoy this Sri Lankan style easy fish fry dish.
